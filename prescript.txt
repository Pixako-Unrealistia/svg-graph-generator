

Simple Chart Builder notions

Fundamental objective

- Implementation of bar, line, scatter, pie chart.
- read settings fron settings.json
- read data from csv file (First line will be header, the rest will be data in format of 'name, amount')
- Being clean and comply with general conventions

- Histogram in console 
- Output as svg
